# Business Recommendations

## Executive Summary

Analysis of 7,043 telecom customers reveals a **26.5% churn rate** with **$139.1K in monthly revenue at risk**. Four priority actions can reduce churn and protect revenue.

---

## Recommendation 1: Convert Month-to-Month Contracts
**Priority:** 🔴 High

| Current State | Target |
|-------------|--------|
| 55% of customers on month-to-month | 40% on month-to-month |
| 43% churn rate for month-to-month | 25% churn rate |

**Action:** Offer 15% discount for switching to annual contract + first month free  
**Impact:** Estimated $42K/month revenue protection

---

## Recommendation 2: Auto-Pay Enrollment
**Priority:** 🔴 High

| Current State | Target |
|-------------|--------|
| 45% churn for electronic check | 15% churn (match credit card) |

**Action:** $5/month discount for auto-pay enrollment + waive setup fee  
**Impact:** Estimated $28K/month revenue protection

---

## Recommendation 3: Security Bundle for Fiber
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium

| Current State | Target |
|-------------|--------|
| 42% churn for fiber without security | 20% churn |

**Action:** Include online security free for 6 months with fiber  
**Impact:** Estimated $18K/month revenue protection

---

## Recommendation 4: New Customer Onboarding
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium

| Current State | Target |
|-------------|--------|
| 47% churn in first 12 months | 30% churn |

**Action:** Dedicated onboarding specialist + 30-day check-in call  
**Impact:** Estimated $15K/month revenue protection

---
